# NYT Writing Quality Rubric

Locked at run start. No mid-run changes.

## Dimensions

### 1. Lede Quality (Weight: 0.20)

**Definition**: How well the opening grabs the reader and sets up what follows.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Generic throat-clearing ("In recent years..."), no hook, could open any article |
| 5-6 | Competent hard lede with the key fact front-loaded, clear but unremarkable |
| 9-10 | Opening that compels the next paragraph; type matches content (anecdotal for features, hard for breaking); first 35 words do real work |

### 2. Narrative Architecture (Weight: 0.20)

**Definition**: Story structure, tension, pacing, and how sections connect.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Flat list of facts, no arc, sections could be reordered without loss |
| 5-6 | Clear logical progression with nut graph, but predictable structure |
| 9-10 | Structure chosen deliberately (hourglass, braided, diamond); tension sustained through "question engine"; kicker resonates and earns its place |

### 3. Voice & Authority (Weight: 0.20)

**Definition**: Tonal range, confidence, distinctiveness, and naturalness of the writing voice.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Sounds like a corporate memo or AI summary; uniform tone throughout |
| 5-6 | Confident analytical voice, consistent but narrow tonal range |
| 9-10 | Distinctive voice with earned authority; tonal shifts feel natural (urgent to contemplative); contractions and sentence variety occur where emphasis demands, not uniformly sprinkled |

### 4. Evidence Integration (Weight: 0.15)

**Definition**: How seamlessly quotes, data, expert opinion, and sourcing are woven into the narrative.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Data dumped in dedicated "findings" paragraphs; no sources named; numbers without context |
| 5-6 | Numbers contextualized (temporal, relative); sources introduced with credentials |
| 9-10 | Quotes, data, and scene detail braided together invisibly; attribution patterns feel natural; paraphrase/quote decisions are strategic |

### 5. Explanatory Clarity (Weight: 0.15)

**Definition**: Making genuinely complex topics accessible without dumbing down.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Jargon unexplained or ideas oversimplified to the point of inaccuracy |
| 5-6 | Complex ideas explained with one clear analogy; jargon introduced with definition |
| 9-10 | Layered explanation (analogy then mechanism); strategic simplification without condescension; reader understands both the "what" and the "why it matters" |

### 6. Anti-AI Quality (Weight: 0.10)

**Definition**: Absence of AI-tell markers and presence of genuinely human editorial qualities.

| Score | Observable Markers |
|-------|-------------------|
| 1-2 | Reads like ChatGPT with a style sheet; uniform sentence rhythm; AI-tell phrases throughout |
| 5-6 | Passes casual human reading; no obvious AI markers but lacks editorial personality |
| 9-10 | Zero AI-tell markers; sentence rhythm genuinely irregular; editorial judgment and skepticism feel earned; shows genuine selectivity about what to emphasize |

## Scoring Rules

- Composite = sum(weight_i * score_i)
- Baseline (v0) anchored at 4-6 per dimension (hard rule)
- Max +1 per dimension per iteration
- Every score must cite specific content from the sample
- State 2-3 weaknesses BEFORE scoring (mandatory)
